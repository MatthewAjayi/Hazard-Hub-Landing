Everything you need to manage workplace safety

Hazard Hub provides a complete solution for reporting, tracking, and resolving workplace hazards.

Instant Hazard Reporting

Take a photo, add notes, select severity, and submit - all in under 2 minutes. GPS location automatically captured for precise tracking.

Smart Assignment System

Supervisors can assign hazards to maintenance teams instantly. Real-time notifications ensure everyone stays informed.

Real-Time Status Tracking

Track hazards from report to resolution. Open → Assigned → Fixed → Verified → Closed. Full visibility at every step.

Push Notifications

Get instant alerts when hazards are assigned, updated, or closed. Tap notifications to jump directly to hazard details.

Automated PDF Reports

Generate professional PDF reports for any hazard. Weekly summary reports automatically created every Monday.

Role-Based Access Control

Supervisors, maintenance, workers, and safety leads - each role has appropriate permissions and access levels.

Photo Documentation

Attach multiple photos to each hazard. Before and after photos for complete documentation. View in full-screen gallery.

Dark Mode Support

Automatic light and dark themes for comfortable viewing in any environment. Fully accessible design.

Advanced Search & Filters

Find hazards quickly with search and filters. Filter by status, severity, or assigned to you. Badge counts for active assignments.

How Hazard Hub Works

From report to resolution in four simple steps

1

Report Hazard

Take a photo, select severity (Low/Medium/High), add notes, and submit. GPS location captured automatically.

2

Assign to Team

Supervisors assign hazards to maintenance team members. Push notification sent instantly with deep link to hazard.

3

Fix & Document

Maintenance uploads fix photos and marks hazard as fixed. All team members can view progress in real-time.

4

Verify & Close

Supervisors verify the fix and close the hazard. PDF report automatically generated for records.
